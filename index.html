Before spending on new tools, I audited what exists. We have production-ready systems that just need to be connected. Here's what I found:

Twitter search → AI intent scoring → qualified leads. Already classifies buyer vs seller, scores intent, suggests outreach. Built for finding prospects.

replier
Chrome extension + AI generates contextual replies on LinkedIn and X. Your co-founder built this — it's solid.

Python programmatic SEO generator. 1000s of static pages from JSON. Built for landing pages at scale.

mail-engine
Cold email infrastructure. Needs a production readiness check, but could be our email backbone.
→ Use it to: Email sequences (pending verification)
03
The Approach

Reuse. Buy. Build.
In That Order.

We don't need to rebuild everything from scratch. We connect what exists, fill critical gaps with cheap tools, and build only what's genuinely new.

We Keep (Existing)
  • signal-tweeter — Twitter lead gen
  • replier — AI social selling
  • viral-pipeline — Content repurposing
  • pSEO — Landing page generator
  • mail-engine — Email (pending check)
  • Your Next.js stack — website
We Buy (Cheap, INR)
  • Zoho CRM — ₹1.2K/mo
  • Apollo — ₹1.5K/mo enrichment
  • Buffer — ₹2K/mo scheduling
  • Crisp — ₹2K/mo live chat
  • Cal.com — Free (demo booking)
  • PostHog — Free (analytics)

Total new spend: ~₹7K/month. Everything else is integration work on tools I've already paid to build.
